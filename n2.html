About EduCareer Pro

EduCareer Pro is a pioneering ed-tech and career consultancy company founded in 2023 in Mohali, Punjab, by two friends, Harsh and Chandan. Their vision was born from a direct understanding of the critical challenges students face: bridging the daunting gap between academic knowledge and the practical skills demanded by the modern workforce.

Driven by a mission to solve these problems, we empower the next generation of professionals with cutting-edge skills and strategic career guidance. We specialize in transforming academic learners into industry-ready candidates through innovative, industry-aligned learning solutions.

Our curriculum, focused intently on emerging technologies and hands-on skill development, is designed to prepare students for high-demand roles in top MNCs and innovative startups worldwide. In a short time, we have successfully transformed the careers of over 50,000 students across India.
